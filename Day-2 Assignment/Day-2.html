BMW

BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke AG) was founded in 1916 in Munich, Germany. The company initially manufactured aircraft engines during World War I. After the war, BMW began producing motorcycles and later entered the automobile industry in 1928. Over the years, BMW became known for its high-quality engineering, luxury vehicles, and performance-oriented cars. Today, BMW is one of the world's leading automobile manufacturers, producing luxury cars, motorcycles, and electric vehicles under the BMW, MINI, and Rolls-Royce brands.

BMW motercycles

BMW Motorcycles, sold under the BMW Motorrad division, are known for their quality, performance, and advanced technology. BMW introduced its first motorcycle, the BMW R 32, in 1923. Today, BMW Motorrad produces a wide range of motorcycles, including sport, touring, adventure, and roadster models, and is recognized as one of the leading premium motorcycle manufacturers in the world.

BMW's First Motorcycle

BMW's first motorcycle was the BMW R 32, introduced in 1923. It was designed by Max Friz and featured a 494 cc two-cylinder boxer engine. The R 32 was notable for its shaft-drive system, which was more reliable and cleaner than chain drives used by many motorcycles of that era. Its innovative design laid the foundation for many future BMW motorcycles.

SUSUKI

Suzuki Motor Corporation was founded in 1909 by Michio Suzuki in Hamamatsu, Japan. The company originally manufactured weaving looms for the textile industry. In the 1950s, Suzuki entered the automotive industry by producing motorcycles and small cars. Today, Suzuki is a globally recognized manufacturer of motorcycles, automobiles, and marine engines, known for its reliable and affordable vehicles.

Susuki motorcycles

Suzuki Motorcycles are produced by Suzuki Motor Corporation and are known for their reliability, performance, and innovation. Suzuki began manufacturing motorcycles in 1952 with the Power Free bicycle motor. Over the years, the company has produced many popular models, including sport bikes, cruisers, and adventure motorcycles. Today, Suzuki is one of the world's leading motorcycle manufacturers, with a strong presence in both racing and everyday transportation.

Hayabusa

The Hayabusa is a high-performance sport motorcycle manufactured by Suzuki. First introduced in 1999, it is famous for its powerful engine, aerodynamic design, and exceptional speed. The Hayabusa quickly became one of the most iconic motorcycles in the world and is widely recognized for its performance, comfort, and advanced technology.
